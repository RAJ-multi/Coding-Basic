Gustavo "Gus" Fring

Gustavo "Gus" Fring, mockingly referred to as The Chicken Man by The Salamancas, is a Chilean-American restaurant entrepreneur and major narcotics distributor who primarily worked in Albuquerque, New Mexico. Originally collaborating with the Mexican drug cartel to distribute their cocaine, Gus eliminated his dependence on the cartel and began distributing methamphetamine himself. Soon enough he became the kingpin of his solo drug empire, which was the most successful drug operation in United States history until his former employee Walter White surpassed it with his own drug empire. Gus is the proprietor of Los Pollos Hermanos, a highly successful fried chicken restaurant chain which he co-founded with his business associate and romantic partner Maximino "Max" Arciniega, and is also the owner of the industrial laundry facility called Lavandería Brillante. Gus uses both of these stores as a front for his meth distribution operation throughout the American southwest, supported by Los Pollos' corporate owners, Madrigal Electromotive GmbH. Gus maintains a positive public image; he is a booster for Albuquerque civic causes, including the local Drug Enforcement Administration (DEA) office; in addition to befriending the special agent in charge, Gus makes large donations to the office's charitable events, and is a member of the hospital board. Underneath this outwardly pleasant front, he is calculatingly ruthless and dangerously Machiavellian in managing his drug empire by using his anti-drug philanthropy to conceal his true nature from the public. He employs a large private security force, which includes several former members of the Chilean secret police, to protect himself and his business interests. Gus is a meticulous planner and a master of strategy, often using his intelligence and resources to outmaneuver his rivals and eliminate threats to his empire. He is also known for his calm and collected demeanor, rarely showing emotion or losing his temper, even in high-pressure situations. Despite his ruthless nature, Gus is also shown to have a strong sense of loyalty and respect for those who work for him, particularly his close associates and employees. He is willing to go to great lengths to protect them and ensure their well-being, as long as they remain loyal and do not pose a threat to his empire.
