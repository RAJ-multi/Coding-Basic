About Brainbook (Ashley Rhiness)

In the video game Dispatch, Brainbook is an elite superhero employed by the Superhero Dispatch Network (SDN). With an IQ exceeding 200, Ashley Rhiness utilizes old-fashioned detective work combined with high-tech encrypted communication analysis. Despite her corporate language and "dork" reputation, she remains a compassionate and vital support for the team.

About Talia al Ghul

Talia al Ghul is a legendary figure in the DC Universe. Known as the "Daughter of the Demon," she is the daughter of Ra's al Ghul and the mother of Damian Wayne. Her life is a constant struggle between her loyalty to the League of Assassins and her complex love for Batman, whom she calls her "Beloved." She is a master strategist and one of the most dangerous martial artists in the world.
